Q: When should I arrive?

The ceremony will begin promptly at 5:00 PM on Friday, September 24, 2027. We recommend arriving 15-30 minutes early at the American Visionary Art Museum to find your seat and settle in!

Q: Where is the venue located?

Both our ceremony and reception will be held at the American Visionary Art Museum (AVAM) in Baltimore. After our ceremony, the museum will be open for guests to explore.

Q: How should I get to the venue?

We're keeping things close! While we won’t have a formal shuttle, our room block hotel, the Royal Sonesta Harbor Court, is just a 12-minute walk from the venue. It’s an easy stroll, but ride-shares (like Uber and Lyft) are also widely available in the area if you prefer to catch a quick ride.

Q: Is this indoors or outdoors?

Our ceremony will be outdoors, with the option of having it in a covered area, if needed. The reception will be inside the Sculpture Barn at AVAM.

Q: What is the dress code?

We would love to see our family and friends get dressed up with us! The dress code is semi-formal / cocktail attire.

Q: Can I bring a plus one or my kids?

Your plus one will be noted when it is time to RSVP. Our wedding is adults only unless we've previously told you otherwise.

Q: How do I submit my RSVP details?

We are tracking responses directly via Google Forms! You can click right here to load up the form. Feel free to send us a note if you have any other questions!
