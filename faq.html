Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before the big day!

Q: When should I arrive?

The ceremony will begin promptly at 5:00 PM on Friday, September 24, 2027. We recommend arriving 15-30 minutes early to find your seat.

Q: Where is the venue located?

Both our ceremony and reception will be held at the American Visionary Art Museum (AVAM) in Baltimore, Maryland.

Q: What is the dress code?

We would love to see our family and friends get dressed up with us! The dress code is semi-formal / cocktail attire.

Q: Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We are having an "unplugged" ceremony. We kindly ask that all phones and cameras be put away so you can be fully present with us. You're welcome to take as many photos as you want during the reception!

Q: Is there parking available at the museum?

Yes! There is parking available, but if you plan on celebrating with a few drinks at the open bar, we highly recommend taking a rideshare (Uber/Lyft) or using our hotel shuttle services.

Q: Can I bring a plus one or my kids?

Due to space limitations at our venue, we are only able to accommodate the guests explicitly listed on your invitation packet. When you initialize your setup on the RSVP page, it will show you your allotted spots!
