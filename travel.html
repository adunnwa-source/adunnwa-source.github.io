Walking Directions

FROM ROYAL SONESTA HOTEL TO AVAM BARN (Approx. 12 Minute Walk)
  1. Exit The Royal Sonesta Harbor Court lobby and head south on Light St toward the Inner Harbor.
  2. Slight left onto the pedestrian path alongside Key Hwy, keeping the harbor water on your left.
  3. Continue walking south down Key Hwy for about half a mile.
  4. Arrive at the American Visionary Art Museum (800 Key Hwy). The entrance to the Sculpture Barn will be on your right.
